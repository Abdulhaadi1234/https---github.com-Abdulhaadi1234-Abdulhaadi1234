The Cedar school

My school name is Cedar school.Cedar School in Karachi is a premier educational institution known for its commitment to academic excellence and holistic development. The school offers a learner-centered and participatory approach, encouraging students to think critically and collaborate. Cedar School provides a comprehensive curriculum, including lower and higher secondary education, enriched with technology and experiential learning programs. The institution also emphasizes student well-being through mentorship, physical education, and sports.The school motto is Learn to Thrive.
